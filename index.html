But what is FLEX-time?
FLEX-time is also commonly known as a Response to Intervention (RTI) program. RTI is a school of thought that enables struggling students to get the help they need during school hours. Schools might have 1-3 "flexible" periods per week, where students can either choose an activity (like art or ping-pong) or have one assigned to them, if they need to bring a grade up.

RTI programs are showing up more and more in schools of all levels around the nation, but many schools try to manage scheduling through tedious spreadsheets - that is, they try and do it manually.
